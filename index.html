About Me

My Bio

I was born and raised in Piqua, Ohio. When people ask me where that is I ask if they know where the intersection of I-70 and I-75 is. No matter what they reply I say "It's no where near there." (Actually it's about 20 miles north of the intersection.) My wife and I graduated from Piqua Central High School the same year. Four years later, we got married. We have two sons, two daughters-in-law, three granddaughters, and two grandsons. I am a retired Air force Captain and former Marine Corps Gunnery Sergeant.

My Tech Experience

I earned an M.S. in Education from Purdue in 1991. It was a concentration in Educational Computing and Instructional Design. I've taught a number of computer programming languages at six different institutes of higher learning. When I was in my graduate program at Purdue, my advisor asked if I would create an application of the Pilot programming language. I used Think C™ on the Mac. The program's final line count was over 6,000 lines. I have a hardcopy of the code if anyone would like to see it. I am a former Web Design instructor with the Eleven Fifty Academy.
